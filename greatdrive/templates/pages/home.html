Founded in 2021 , The Great Drive is an endurance drive during which we drive long distances to donate to children’s homes that are sometimes located in remote and forgotten places and we endeavour to provide a sustainable solution to their basic needs.

Our logo stretches out of its boundary, just like we do. We’re not confined. We stretch our wings. We explore, we venture out to the unknown. We reach out to help the less fortunate. We stretch our hands out in friendship towards each other and build strong ties.
